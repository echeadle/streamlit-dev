Creating & Managing an Account

Creating a Note & Nib Co. account allows you to track orders, save preferences, and earn rewards.

- Sign Up: Click "Create Account" on our website, enter your email and set a password.
- Logging In: Use your registered email and password to access your account.
- Password Reset: If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

Your account keeps all your stationery needs organized in one place!
