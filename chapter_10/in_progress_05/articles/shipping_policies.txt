Shipping Policies & Timeframes

We offer fast and reliable shipping for all Note & Nib Co. products. Here’s what you need to know:

- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
- Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days.
- International Shipping: Varies by location, typically 7-14 business days.

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. If you experience delays, our support team is here to help.
